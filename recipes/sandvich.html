The Sandvich

An Eastern European favorite, the aptly named "sandvich" is composed of ingredients sourced from entirely different parts of the world. This succulent dish is the choice of many warfighters, especially those weilding impressive weaponry. It's flavors will leave you feeling stronger and more capable, and is guaranteed to ruin all other sandwiches for you.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Maltese Sourdough Bread
  • 3-6 count sliced Vienna black forest ham
  • 1 count Sliced Jarlsberg Norwegian cheese
  • 3 count Sliced KT beefsteak tomatoes or Florence heirloom tomatoes
  • 1-3 leaves of iceberg lettuce
  • 2 tbsp Deep American South Bluetop mayonnaise
  • 1 count Spanish stuffed pimento queen olive

Steps

  1. Slice your bread, ham, cheese, and tomotoes.
  2. Fold and lay ham onto bottom of bread.
  3. Layer cheese, tomotoes, and lettuce on top.
  4. Complete with second slice of sourdough.
  5. Insert toothpick with olive as garnish.
  6. Plate and enjoy or pack in lunch to enjoy on the go!

Important to note: toasting the bread is not advised due to the potential for crubs to find their way into hard to reach/clean areas, such as the multi-barrel gas system of your standard minigun.
